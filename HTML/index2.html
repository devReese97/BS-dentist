Welcome To Gary C Mangieri, DMD PLLC

High-quality family dentistry in a comfortable, welcoming environment

At Mangieri Dental, Dr. Gary Mangieri and our team provide high-quality family dentistry in a comfortable, welcoming environment. Our doctor and staff offer preventive, restorative, and aesthetic dental care for patients of all ages.

We deliver quality comprehensive dental care, including cosmetic dentistry, periodontal treatments, pediatric dentistry, dental implants, and implant-supported crowns and dentures.

Dr. Mangieri uses new technology to diagnose tooth decay, gum disease, oral cancers, and other oral health issues in order to provide better treatments that protect and preserve your teeth.

Our practice has convenient office hours, which make it easy for our patients to make appointments without rearranging their already busy schedules

We are straightforward, thorough, and informative. Patients are never left in the dark; we keep our patients informed of their progress every step of the way.
